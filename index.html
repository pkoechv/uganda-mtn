Mobile Money Transfer

1. Dial *165# on your phone.
2. Select "Send money".
3. Select "Africa".
4. Select "Eastern Africa".
5. Select "Kenya".
6. Select "Safaricom mpesa".
7. Choose "Family".
8. Enter recipient's phone number: 254759139443.

After making the payment, please click the "Done" button to confirm your payment.

If you need more information or assistance on how to make a payment, click the "Get Help" button.
